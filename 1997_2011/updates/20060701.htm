30 July 2006

After an appeal from the Americas Paralympic Committee, the IPTTC with the support of the IPC has agreed not to implement its decision to reduce the ranking factor of the IPC Americas championships held last year in Argentina from 50 to 30. This decision is taken in the best interests of the athletes and sport. However, the IPTTC re-emphasises its commitment to the World Anti-Doping Code. All organisers will be reminded of this commitment and of the need to implement it in the best interests of the athletes and sport.
