Corrections of 2015-12-01 ranking lists


A player has reported a problem regarding matches won against low rated players in singles in Thailand Open.

We investigated and found a bug in ranking software: in factor 40 or more tournaments, a win in singles with a rating difference greater than 250 was counted as 1 point for winner which was wrong because it has to be multiplied by 1.5 weight and rounded to 2 similarly to what is done for a win against unrated player.

So we adjusted ratings accordingly for tournaments included in tournament credit period for Rio: 2014 and 2015.

The details of the adjustments can be found in this table which lists the opponents and tournaments

Please note that this applies only to single events in factor 40 or more tournaments when the rating difference between players is greater than 250

We're sorry that this mistake has been discovered so late.
